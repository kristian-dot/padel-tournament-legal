Privacy Policy for Padel Tournament Effective date: [April 7, 2026] Padel Tournament respects your privacy. Padel Tournament is designed for local and personal use. The app helps users create padel tournaments, track scores, view standings, and capture winner photos for sharing. Information Collection and Use Padel Tournament does not require account creation. Padel Tournament does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data with the developer or third parties. All tournament data created in the app is stored locally on the user's device. Camera Permission Padel Tournament may request access to the device camera only when the user chooses to take a winner photo from within the app. The camera is used solely for that feature. Photos are not uploaded to any server by the app. Storage and Media Access Padel Tournament may access device storage or media only as needed to save or share winner photos created by the user. This access is used only to support app functionality selected by the user. Data Sharing Padel Tournament does not share user data with third parties. Data Retention Because app data is stored locally on the user's device, the user controls retention by keeping or deleting the app and its local data. Children's Privacy Padel Tournament is not directed specifically to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Security Padel Tournament does not operate a backend server for user data. Tournament data remains on the user's device. Changes to This Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
